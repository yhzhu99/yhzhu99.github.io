[About Me] I'm currently a master student under the supervision of Prof. Chengwei Pan at Beihang University and Prof. Liantao Ma at Peking University. Additionally, I work closely with Junyi Gao from HDR UK. My research primarily focuses on AI for Healthcare, particularly in the areas of data mining within electronic health records (EHR) and developing multimodal foundation models for healthcare applications.
